WeatherSense is a web app that uses advanced machine learning algorithms to predict weather and later on future weathers based on the weather forecast data – precipitation, wind speed, humidity, temperature, maximum temperature, and cloud cover – over the next 7 days. The fundamental objective is to empower individuals, communities, and government agencies with real-time insights, enabling informed decision-making, disaster preparedness, and enhanced public safety. This platform would transcend traditional weather forecasting applications by delivering comprehensive, real-time weather insights, ensuring users are informed, prepared, and ready to make informed decisions in a rapidly changing climate. Hence, it would operate at the intersection of meteorology, data analysis, and user-friendly technology, designed to make vital information accessible, thereby enhancing individual and community resilience in the face of dynamic environmental conditions.
